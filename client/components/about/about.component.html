We are happy to announce the first MTA Hackathon
which will be held at the Academic Tel Aviv Yafo College.
on April 6th 2018.

If you don't have any idea what the hell a Hackathon is ,we will explain it briefly.
A Hackathon is an event when programmers and designers meet to do collaborative computer programming
and provide a venue for self-expression and creativity through technology.

The theme of this year's competition is smart education

So, you're probably sitting down in your cozy chair, scratching your head wondering what the hell we want from you?
We're basically giving you the option to do whatever you want!
Just try to make our education better.
